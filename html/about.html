Most event organizers are faced with the need to work with loads of different technologies for the management of people and entities who participate in their events.

cBooking gathers them all in a single plataform, providing the organizer with tools to register, track and manage whoever is going to participate in their event.

A transparent and free tool

Sponsors, Partners together

At cBooking we work to deliver a free tool that can promote the organization of events, by supporting its logistics and registrations processes.

Whoever is participating in an event can register himself and reach other available events. To make it as smooth as possible for you, dear organizer: we process your data, track registrations, and provide you with a private dashboard for the management of the event.
